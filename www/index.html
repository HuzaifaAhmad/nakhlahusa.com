Arabic Forensics

تعلم اللغة العربية بدقة و تعمق يودي إلى فهم النصوص من القران والسنة من حيث دلالتها على الأحكام والمواعظ والحكم

Linguistics is the systematic, scientific study of language. "Forensic" linguistics refers to linguistics applied to any use of language with legal relevance.
The term "Arabic Forensics " refers to an in depth study of the Arabic language that leads to the understanding of texts from Quran and Sunnah in terms of their indication to various rulings, spiritual counsels and maxims.
